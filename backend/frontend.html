Personalized Eco Tips

Small changes. Big impact.

💡

Switch to LED bulbs

Can save up to 80% electricity. Replace just 5 bulbs and cut 120 kg CO₂/year.

🚲

Use public transport 3 days/week

Reduces transport emissions by 45%. Tamil Nadu buses are already electric in many routes!

🥗

Go meat-free twice a week

Cuts diet emissions by 30%. Try local millets and greens from your district.

🌱

Plant a tree this month

Neem or Mango saplings are perfect for Tamil Nadu climate. Offset 22 kg CO₂ annually.

🛍️

Refuse single-use plastic

Carry your own cloth bag and steel bottle. Saves 1.5 kg plastic waste per month.

🔌

Unplug devices at night

Phantom load can be 10% of your bill. Simple habit = big savings.
